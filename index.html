How it works

01
Set a goal
Pick what you're committing to. Work out every day. Read for 30 minutes. Quit scrolling at midnight. Whatever it is, make it real.
02
Create a pact
Set your duration and lock in your check-in window, the time each day you prove you showed up. Miss it and your pact knows. Hit it and the streak grows.
03
Invite your people
Share a join code or find them on Pact and request them into your pact. Friends, rivals, strangers who get it. Whoever keeps you honest.
04
Follow through
Check in daily and post your proof. Every pact has its own leaderboard so everyone in it knows exactly who's putting in the work and who isn't.
