Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Pact ("we," "us," "our") operates the Pact mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We are based in the United Kingdom and process personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Pact is a social accountability app that helps users make commitments ("pacts"), invite friends as accountability partners, check in daily, and communicate in group chats.

Contact: yassin@pactapp.co.uk or ali@pactapp.co.uk

2. Information We Collect

When you use Pact, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, name
  • Profile information: username, bio, birthday
  • Pact and activity data: the pacts you create or join, and your daily check-in records
  • Communications: messages you send in group chats
  • Photos: images you upload as proof of check-ins
  • Usage data: basic technical information needed to operate the App (e.g. device type, app version), collected automatically as part of standard app operation

We do not collect more than what is needed to run the App's core features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Operate core App features: pacts, daily check-ins, accountability tracking, and group chat
  • Show your check-ins and progress to the accountability partners/groups you've chosen to share them with
  • Maintain the safety and integrity of the App, including responding to reports of abuse (see our Terms of Service)
  • Communicate with you about your account or the App, when necessary

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not use it for third-party advertising.

4. How Your Information Is Stored

Pact is built on Supabase, a third-party backend infrastructure provider, which stores and processes the data described above on our behalf. Supabase acts as our data processor and maintains its own security safeguards. We do not share your data with any other third party except as required to operate the App (via Supabase) or as required by law.

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, we delete your associated data as described in Section 7 below, except where retention is required for legal, security, or fraud-prevention purposes.

6. Children's Privacy

Pact is intended for users aged 16 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If we become aware that a user under 16 has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete it. If you believe a user under 16 is using Pact, please contact us at the email above.

7. Your Rights and Account Deletion

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data
  • Object to or restrict certain processing
  • Data portability, where applicable

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time directly in the App: go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes your account information, profile, pact and check-in history, and chat messages from our systems.

To exercise any other rights, or if you have questions about your data, contact us at yassin@pactapp.co.uk or ali@pactapp.co.uk. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the UK Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

8. Data Security

We rely on Supabase's security infrastructure (encryption in transit and at rest, access controls) to protect your data. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, but we take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. If we make material changes, we will notify you through the App or by other reasonable means. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

Questions about this Privacy Policy or your data can be sent to: yassin@pactapp.co.uk or ali@pactapp.co.uk
