Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Pact ("we," "us," "our") operates the Pact mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and what rights you have. We are based in the United Kingdom and process personal data in accordance with the UK GDPR and the Data Protection Act 2018.

1. Who We Are

Pact is a social accountability app that helps users make commitments ("pacts"), invite friends as accountability partners, check in daily, and communicate in group chats.

Contact: yassin@pactapp.co.uk or ali@pactapp.co.uk

2. Information We Collect

When you use Pact, we collect:

  • Account information: email address, name
  • Profile information: username, bio, birthday
  • Pact and activity data: the pacts you create or join, and your daily check-in records
  • Communications: messages you send in group chats
  • Photos: images you upload as proof of check-ins
  • Usage data: basic technical information needed to operate the App (e.g. device type, app version), collected automatically as part of standard app operation

We do not collect more than what is needed to run the App's core features.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Operate core App features: pacts, daily check-ins, accountability
